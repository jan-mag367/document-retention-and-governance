# Review and archive checklist (draft)

## Monthly
- Check external sharing links
- Review new folders/sites for correct permissions
- Confirm owners list is current

## Quarterly
- Permissions review for key libraries (governance/finance)
- Clean up duplicates and confirm source-of-truth locations
- Archive completed project/campaign folders

## Annually
- Review retention schedule guidance
- Confirm templates are current
- Update naming conventions if needed
