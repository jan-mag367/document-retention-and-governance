# Retention schedule template (draft)

This is a generic template to guide retention planning. Adjust for your organisation's policies and legal requirements.

## Categories
| Category | Examples | Suggested retention | Notes |
|---------|----------|---------------------|------|
| Governance records | agendas, minutes, decisions | X years | keep final signed/approved copies |
| Financial records | budgets, expense summaries | X years | restricted access |
| Training materials | guides, onboarding docs | while current + X years | review annually |
| Campaign materials | plans, leaflets, comms drafts | X years | archive after campaign ends |
| General admin | checklists, internal docs | X years | review periodically |
